# DecodeLabs – The Empathy Map

## Project Overview

The Empathy Map is a UX research project focused on understanding the needs, goals, behaviors, and challenges of busy office professionals before designing a fitness application.

## Problem Statement

Busy professionals often struggle to maintain fitness due to long working hours, stress, and lack of time for exercise.

## Objectives

- Define the target audience
- Create user personas
- Build an empathy map
- Identify user pain points

## Target Audience

**Busy Office Professionals**
- Age: 22–45 years
- Long working hours
- Limited time for fitness
- Health-conscious but time-constrained

## User Personas

### Rahul
A 29-year-old software engineer who wants quick and easy workouts to stay healthy and reduce stress.

### Priya
A 32-year-old HR manager who wants simple and flexible workouts to stay active and maintain fitness.

## Key Pain Points

- Lack of time for exercise
- Work-related stress
- Low motivation
- Inconsistent workout routines

## Tools Used

- Figma
- UX Research Methods

## Conclusion

This project helped identify the needs and challenges of busy office professionals through user personas and empathy mapping. The insights gathered provide a strong foundation for designing a user-centered fitness application.
